ABOUT THE PROJECT

About the Project

A stake pool is created and operated by The WiFi Project. Investors can delegate Cardano currency to the pool. Fees are collected by The WiFi Project to pay for and maintain free WiFi hotspots. The remainder of profits are paid out to delegators
